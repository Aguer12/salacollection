About Us

Welcome to Sala Collection, your number one source for all outfits of your choice including slim fit suites, African wear and bags for ladies. We are dedicated to giving you the very best of our products with focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. We are located in Nakuru Kenya at Shawmut plaza along Timber Mill Road opposite KPLC Depot. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.

I'm here to make you look good

Sala Collection has always been driven by providing best slim suites and African wear to all fashionistas and all those who love looking nice and smart. We have come a long way from the beginning of SalaCollection and now we serve customers from all over African while based in East Africa. Sala Collection is thrilled to be part of the Fashion Industry.
